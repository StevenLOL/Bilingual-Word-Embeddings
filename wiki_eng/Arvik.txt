Owrang (Persian: اورنگ‎, also Romanized as Avarang and Avrang; also known as Arvik)[1] is a village in Azghan Rural District, in the Central District of Ahar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 427, in 110 families.[2]
